Project Proposal

Our project focuses on the urgent problem of deforestation, which harms local livelihoods, farms, and the environment. We aim to raise awareness and take action through tree planting, forest protection, and community involvement. By restoring forest areas and promoting sustainable land use, we hope to support biodiversity and ensure a greener future for rural communities.
